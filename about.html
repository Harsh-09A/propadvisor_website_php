About PropAdvisor

ABOUT US

Shaping Real Estate Since 2017

At PropAdvisor, we simplify real estate for developers, families, and investors (including NRIs) in Navi Mumbai. Since 2017, we have been partnering with developers through exclusive mandates to market and sell projects effectively, while also guiding buyers to their dream homes and helping investors make profitable decisions. With strong industry networks, local expertise, and a commitment to transparency, we are more than consultants, we are your trusted property advisors.

We understand that property decisions come with doubts, from project sales challenges for developers to hidden costs and complex paperwork for buyers and investors. At PropAdvisor, we simplify every step of the property journey. With over 22 nationalities and 25+ languages represented, we’re committed to making the real estate experience seamless and rewarding.

As Navi Mumbai’s trusted partner in Sole Selling and Mandate Services, PropAdvisor brings expertise in sales management and real estate advisory under one roof. Our team of specialists in finance, legal, and real estate ensures projects are executed seamlessly, from budget-friendly homes priced at ₹7 Lakhs to luxury spaces worth ₹28 Crores.

Operating across Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, we make property decisions simpler for developers through actionable market insights, and for buyers and investors through a clear and comprehensive approach. Developers gain stronger project positioning, while homebuyers discover spaces that fit their lifestyle.

For developers, we go beyond sales. We offer actionable market insights into buyer preferences, pricing strategies, and design trends. With PropAdvisor, you don’t just sell projects, you create experiences that buyers value.

Partner with us for smarter sales, stronger insights, and seamless real estate journeys .
