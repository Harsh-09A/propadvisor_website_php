Mid-Income Housing Project in Ulwe

The Challenge:

A mid-income housing project in Ulwe, Navi Mumbai, was struggling to generate traction during its pre-launch phase. Despite having a strong location advantage with proximity to major transit routes and growth potential, the project faced slower-than-expected bookings. Buyers were hesitant due to pricing concerns and limited awareness in a highly competitive market.

Our Approach:

  • Redesigned the sales pitch by highlighting Ulwe’s infrastructure growth and long-term ROI potential.
  • Optimized the pricing strategy to align with buyer expectations while maintaining developer profitability.
  • Launched a 360° marketing plan, combining targeted digital campaigns, on-ground activations, and channel partner engagement.
  • Positioned the project as the best value-for-money investment in Ulwe’s growing residential hub.

The Results:

  • Achieved 60% inventory sales within the first 3 months of the pre-launch campaign.
  • Attracted both end-users and investors by balancing lifestyle appeal with investment growth narratives.
  • Enhanced the developer’s credibility in the Ulwe micro-market, establishing trust for future launches.
